# Ode to Silence

Silence

Awkward Silence

Uncomfortable Silence

Annoying Silence

Can't stand Silence

"I said shut up!" Silence

"Did you hear what I said?" Silence

"Why is it so quiet?" Silence

"Am I Lost?" Silence

Toyota Prius Silence

Middle of nowhere Silence

Daydream Silence

Much needed Silence

Uninterrupted Silence

Comforting Silence

Loving Silence

At peace Silence

Rest In Peace Silence

Silence
